BMW 5 Series

The BMW 5-Series is priced at Rs 72.9 lakh (ex-showroom), and can be had in a sole 530Li M Sport variant.BMW launched the eight-generation 5 Series in India at Rs 72.9 lakh (ex-showroom). It is offered in a single 530Li variant and can be opted with a sole turbo-petrol engine. The 5 Series comes in the long-wheelbase version for the first time, and offers an upmarket interior as well as several top-notch convenience and safety features.
